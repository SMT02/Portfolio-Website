Magnolia -AI Shopping Assistant

For my senior capstone project at UTC, I worked alongside a group to build an AI shopping assistant app sponsored by Capgemini which aimed at streamlining the shopping process by providing the ability to create lists with AI assistance and for real-time directions with the most efficient route.

Elysian Furnishings -Premium Furniture Store

Elysian Furnishings was a group project at UTC which employed React, HTML, CSS, and MySQL to create a premium online furniture store where users could view products, create an account and add items to wishlist and cart, and leave reviews.

Other features include a light/dark mode and the ability for vendors to create an account and upload products.
